More

Since I can remember, figuring out how things work and how new technology is always evolving, has constantly influenced and intrigued me. After working in sales for a few years I decided to take out all of my savings and humbly move back in with my parents to attend a full immersion web development bootcamp. My new found passion for coding combined with my desire to learn pushed me to pursue a carreer in web development.
